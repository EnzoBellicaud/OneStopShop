About UniPortal

One hub for all six universities

UniPortal brings together master thesis projects, internships, job openings, and courses from six leading Swedish universities into a single, easy-to-navigate platform.

Students and faculty no longer need to visit each university's website separately. We aggregate and update listings in real time, so you always see the latest opportunities in one place.

6 universities All partner data in one feed
